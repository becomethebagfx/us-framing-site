Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commercial wood framing with US Framing.

US Framing specializes in large-scale multi-family residential, student housing, senior and assisted living, commercial, and mixed-use projects. We frame 15M+ square feet annually across the United States, handling projects from 50 to 800+ units. Our sweet spot is Type III and Type V multi-family projects, but we also take on commercial and CLT/mass timber work.

Our MiTek Sapphire BIM modeling creates a complete 3D structural model before construction begins. This enables clash detection with other trades, material optimization reducing waste by up to 15%, accurate cost estimation, and faster on-site production because crews work from precise digital plans. BIM modeling typically saves 10-20% on project costs by catching issues before they become expensive field problems.

Takeover as a Service is our rapid response program for stalled or troubled framing projects. We can mobilize crews within 48 hours anywhere in the US, assess the current state, stabilize the project, and drive it to completion. Whether the previous framer walked off, fell behind schedule, or delivered substandard work, our experienced teams have the skills and capacity to get your project back on track.

Cross-Laminated Timber (CLT) is an engineered wood product made by layering boards at right angles and bonding them with structural adhesive. CLT buildings can reach up to 18 stories under the 2021 IBC code, with engineered hybrid systems reaching 30 floors. CLT offers faster construction times (up to 25% quicker), a 75% lower carbon footprint than steel or concrete, and beautiful exposed wood aesthetics that command premium rents.

US Framing operates nationwide with regional teams covering the Atlantic, Northeast, Midwest, West, South, Southeast, and Florida regions. Our national scale means consistent quality and competitive pricing wherever your project is located. We maintain regional offices and crew bases to ensure rapid mobilization and local expertise in every market we serve.

Quality is built into our 7-step US Framing Way methodology. Every project receives daily inspections, dedicated project managers, and real-time progress tracking via Fieldwire. Our safety record speaks for itself with an industry-leading 0.5 EMR (Experience Modification Rate), well below the industry average of 1.0. We invest heavily in crew training, PPE, and site safety programs to protect our people and your investment.
