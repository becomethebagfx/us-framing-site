About US Framing

The nation's largest multi-family wood framing group, delivering 15M+ sqft of precision framing every year across the United States.

Our Story

From a Small Crew to a National Force

US Framing International was founded in the early 1990s by Thomas English with a single framing crew and a commitment to doing things right. What began as a small operation framing houses in Kentucky has grown into the nation's largest multi-family wood framing group, delivering over 15 million square feet of production framing annually.

Based in Pewee Valley, Kentucky, we built our reputation on three principles: uncompromising quality, a relentless focus on safety, and a willingness to adopt the latest technology before the rest of the industry catches up. Our early investment in BIM technology and pre-design capabilities set us apart from traditional framing contractors and allowed us to take on increasingly complex projects.

Today, with 201-500 employees and approximately $14M in annual revenue, US Framing serves every major market in the United States. From the Atlantic coast to the West, from Florida to the Midwest, our regional teams deliver consistent quality backed by national resources and decades of expertise.

Our Mission

"To deliver the highest quality wood framing services through innovative technology, skilled craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to safety and client satisfaction."

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth

Early 1990s

Founded by Thomas English

Thomas English starts US Framing International with a small crew framing houses in Kentucky, building a reputation for quality craftsmanship and reliability.

Late 1990s

Multi-Family Market Expansion

Recognizing the opportunity in multi-family construction, US Framing pivots from single-family homes to large-scale apartment and condominium projects.

2005

BIM Technology Adoption

US Framing becomes an early adopter of BIM technology with MiTek Sapphire, revolutionizing pre-design and reducing material waste across all projects.

2010

100+ Employee Milestone

The company surpasses 100 employees, establishing itself as one of the premier wood framing contractors in the Midwest and Southeast.

2015

Nationwide Expansion Begins

US Framing expands beyond its home markets, establishing regional operations across the Atlantic, Northeast, South, Southeast, West, and Florida.

2018

CLT / Mass Timber Division

Launches a dedicated Cross-Laminated Timber and mass timber division, positioning the company at the forefront of sustainable construction technology.

2020

US Family of Companies Partnership

The US Family of Companies partnership is formalized, integrating US Framing with US Drywall, US Exteriors, US Interiors, and US Development for framing-through-finish capability.

2024

15M+ Sqft Annual Capacity

US Framing achieves 15 million+ square feet of annual framing capacity, solidifying its position as the nation's largest multi-family wood framing group.

2026

Serving All Major US Markets

With regional teams spanning the Atlantic, Northeast, Midwest, West, South, Southeast, and Florida, US Framing delivers nationwide coverage with local expertise.
